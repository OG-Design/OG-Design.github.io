About - Maui

Maui's creation

Maui was constructed by two volcanoes (“Puu Kukui” and “Haleakala”)

The island appeared approximately 750_000 years ago, and it continues to grow to this day as Pu’u Kukui is still active, it’s in fact the most active volcano on earth.

ethnicity

The island was first populated by the Polynesians about 1300 years ago (” approximately 700AD”). Then later populated by European settlers in the 19th century.

The following decades the ethnically Hawaiian population declined to approximately 24 000 in 1920 from a massive 300 - 600 000 people in the 1770’s.

Most of the decline was caused by diseases brought by the Europeans in 1778, to which the natives had no immunity or resistance due to never having had any contact with them before.

The largest city

Kahului

With a population of aproximately 27_000, Kahului meets the criteria of being the largest city in Maui.

People flock to Kahului and with good reason, Kahalui's massive beach provides a stunning view for locals and tourists alike.
